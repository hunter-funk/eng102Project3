Some of our favorite games from the past decade have suffered due to poor management and dishonest advertisements. This includes false promises, staged or faked gameplay reveals, celebrity endorsement, charismatic spokespeople, rushed developments, and abysmal workplace environments. At Tanuki Games we strive to prevent those misdeeds and intend to make our business yours.
Some of our favorite games have succeeded from a transparent and open relationship with the public. We hope to replicate their success and build similar fanbase interaction. Through Kickstarter rewards, developer streams, and player feedback, we hope you stick with us for the best product possible.
