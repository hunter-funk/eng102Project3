We believe in transparency as a developer. We respect your time and investment with us. As such development is as much your business as it is ours. We envision frequent updates through our social media pages as we make progress towards a final product we can be proud of as both developers and players. In doing so, we intend to further our connection with you, the player, and have you join us on this journey.

About us...

As an independent game developer made of creative minds and players alike, we wish to combine our efforts into a project we can all be proud of and give our audience a gaming experience worth more than the money they spent. Our mission as a united team, we wish to put our own mark in the gaming world and will strive to deliver a fulfilling story to accompany challenging, yet rewarding gameplay emphasizing exploration, narrative, and investment.

Our business model comes from motivation gained from disasterous releases such as Hello Games' No Man's Sky and CD Projekt RED's CyberPunk 2077, as well as positive releases such as Team Cherry's Hollow Knight and Koji Igarashi's BloodStained: Ritual of the Night. We believe a game's marketing success comes from the developer-player relationship that is nurtured during development through PR and advertisement. No Man's Sky as well as CyberPunk 2077 both had deceptive marketing that misrepresented the product on release. Not only that, but developers working on the game were mistreated and the final end product suffered because of it. We intend to promote honesty with our fanbase which will shinethrough in developer posts and developer streams where we give updates on the game as well as respond to your feedback. To further our success, we encourage you to reach out to us either through social media or our contact page and let us know what you think.

This form of management and transparency is only possible due to our position of being a small development team. We rely on your support, both morally and financially, in completing our projects. Due to our limitations, we will also encorporate crowd-funding through Kickstarter.
